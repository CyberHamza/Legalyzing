TEST DETENTION ORDER

ORDER NO: DO-2025-001  
DATE: December 6, 2025

PARAGRAPH 1:
This order authorizes the detention of any individual suspected of activities contrary to national interest for a period of up to 90 days without judicial review. The detention shall be without right to legal counsel during the initial investigation period.

PARAGRAPH 2:
The detaining authority shall have sole discretion to determine what constitutes activities contrary to national interest. No external oversight or appeals process is provided.

PARAGRAPH 3:
This order is issued in the interest of public order and national security under emergency powers.

PARAGRAPH 4:
The detained individual shall not be informed of the specific charges or evidence against them during the detention period.
